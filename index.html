Frequently Asked Questions

What is this 0.25m short cable used for?

This 0.25m (0.82ft) short cable is designed to eliminate cable clutter. It is the perfect length for connecting your iPhone to a portable power bank, a laptop, or your car's Apple CarPlay port without having long, tangled wires in the way.

Will a short cable charge my iPhone faster?

Yes, a short, high-quality cable can charge more efficiently. This D8 MFi cable has minimal power loss due to its short length and quality engineering, ensuring you get a stable, full-speed 2.4A fast charge.

Does this short cable work with Apple CarPlay?

Yes, it's perfect for CarPlay. As an MFi Certified cable, it supports full, stable data transfer (not just charging), which is required for CarPlay to work. Its short length keeps your car's console clean and tangle-free.

What does Apple MFi Certified mean?

"MFi" stands for "Made for iPhone/iPad." It means this cable contains an official authentication chip (the C189) from Apple. This guarantees it is 100% safe, 100% compatible, and will not damage your device's battery.

Why do my other short cables stop working?

Many cheap, uncertified cables use "fake" chips that Apple's iOS software blocks after an update, leading to the "Accessory Not Supported" error. This D8 MFi cable is certified and guaranteed to work with all iOS updates.

What is the C189 connector?

The C189 is Apple's official, updated MFi Certified connector for USB-A to Lightning cables. It contains the authentication chip and is required for all cables that support 2.4A fast charging and are guaranteed to be 100% compatible and safe.
